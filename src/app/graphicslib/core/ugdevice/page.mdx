<H2 id="getQueue">getQueue</H2>

Retrieve a queue of a specific type from the logical device

<CodeTabs
  uhc={`VkQueue getQueue(VkPhysicalDevice device, VkDevice logicalDevice, VkQueueFlagBits queueType);`}
  cpp={`VkQueue getQueue(VkPhysicalDevice device, VkDevice logicalDevice, VkQueueFlagBits queueType);`}
/>

**Parameters**
- `device` — Physical device used during queue family lookup
- `logicalDevice` — Logical device to retrieve the queue from
- `queueType` — Queue capability flag identifying the desired queue

**Returns** — Queue handle

---

<H2 id="getPresentationQueue">getPresentationQueue</H2>

Retrieve the presentation queue from the logical device

<CodeTabs
  uhc={`VkQueue getPresentationQueue(VkPhysicalDevice device, VkSurfaceKHR surface, VkDevice logicalDevice);`}
  cpp={`VkQueue getPresentationQueue(VkPhysicalDevice device, VkSurfaceKHR surface, VkDevice logicalDevice);`}
/>

**Parameters**
- `device` — Physical device used for surface support checks
- `surface` — Surface to present to
- `logicalDevice` — Logical device to retrieve the queue from

**Returns** — Presentation queue handle

---

<H2 id="getSurfaceSupport">getSurfaceSupport</H2>

Check whether a physical device supports presentation to a surface

<CodeTabs
  uhc={`U32 getSurfaceSupport(VkPhysicalDevice device, VkSurfaceKHR surface);`}
  cpp={`unsigned int getSurfaceSupport(VkPhysicalDevice device, VkSurfaceKHR surface);`}
/>

**Parameters**
- `device` — Physical device to check
- `surface` — Surface to check presentation support for

**Returns** — Non-zero queue family index if supported
